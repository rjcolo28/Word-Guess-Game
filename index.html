Hangman's Shanty

You're a pirate captain whose ship been captured by the Royal Navy. You and your mates face execution at the gallows, but the commodore gives you a chance to save each member of your crew. All you have to do is guess the word he is thinking. Guess correctly within ten attempts and you save your mate. Lose and he shall hang in the gallows.
